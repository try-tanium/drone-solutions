Singhs Launch New Drone Business

By Kurt Johnson on Tuesday, March 11, 2025

Having gained confidence and experience with aerial photography when he helped launch a statewide drone program for the Nebraska State Patrol, Ravi Singh has ventured into the private sector with his wife by launching a new business called Innovative Drone Solutions.

A member of the State Patrol since 2015, Trooper Singh lives in Aurora and is stationed out of the Grand Island office. He reflected on his role in launching a new program for the State Patrol in hopes of utilizing technology to document accident scenes and speed up road closure times.

Nebraska State Patrol Drone Program Success

July 7, 2022

The Nebraska State Patrol has reported significant time and cost savings with their drone program, cutting accident investigation time from 2.5 hours to just 53 minutes on average.

Trooper Ravi Singh, who helped implement the drone program, demonstrated the technology to Governor Pete Ricketts, who called it "very cool" and "a game changer" for the patrol.
